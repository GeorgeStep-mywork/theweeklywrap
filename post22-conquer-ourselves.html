Conquer Ourselves

"It is not the mountain we conquer, but ourselves." Edmund Hillary

Posted by Annie K Oommen on May 25, 2021

A prominent problem which we face today is the absence of goals. People do not know what they are passionate about and what they want to achieve in life. To be a conqueror, it is essential to have a goal in your life as the first step.

"It is not the mountain we conquer, but ourselves." Edmund Hillary

Confidence and determination are two powerful words which have to be etched into the minds of an individual who wants to accomplish anything in life. A person lacks confidence when he or she is in doubt. This may be the reason for dissatisfaction and why simple daily tasks may seem to be challenging. Every action with determination will automatically invite confidence. This confidence eventually accumulates power in every action of ours. When there is the thought ‘ I can do it’, there is surely a power to it. Then there will not be any distractions, comparisons etc and all our resources would be put to the maximum use to accomplish the task.

When there is a challenge which brings out a negative response, there is an immediate desire to move away from that situation so that the negativity vanishes. By trying to ignore the negativity, you are only making yourself weaker. The real challenge is not the situation itself, but the expression of weakness within. The one who has confidence to face this weakness and work on it is able to bring change and find progress in every step. Thereon there will be an absence of the feeling of helplessness and you have full control of the situation. We need to accept both positive and negative situations to achieve our goal. The ability to learn from each situation and move forward constantly with passion will help you grow exponentially.

The one who is victorious enables others to be victorious too. They have an ability to give confidence to others and encourage them to achieve something better. You make them adapt better to stress and adversity by building resilience. When you help someone, it gives you a feeling of contentment and you tend to experience the presence of positive energy in the environment. And, moreover, you see yourself as a better person.
